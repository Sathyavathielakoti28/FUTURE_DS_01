# Superstore Sales Dashboard Analysis

## Project Overview

This project presents a Power BI dashboard developed to analyze Superstore sales performance. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends, profitability, customer segments, product performance, and regional contributions.

The goal is to support data-driven decision-making by identifying business opportunities and areas requiring improvement.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Compare regional business performance.
- Understand customer segment contributions.
- Discover profitability improvement opportunities.

---

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Total Quantity Sold

---

## Key Insights

- Sales show clear seasonal trends.
- Certain categories drive the majority of revenue.
- Profitability varies significantly across categories and regions.
- A few products contribute heavily to total sales.
- High sales do not always result in high profits.

---

## Recommendations

- Focus on high-performing regions.
- Optimize pricing and discount strategies.
- Promote high-margin products.
- Improve performance in low-profit categories.
- Strengthen customer retention efforts.
